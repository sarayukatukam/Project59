school achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment pollution

The participants Master Advay Matapurkar and Master Aarav Patel from STD IX first qualified in the elimination round based on the topic ‘World’s destruction of forests is justified for the human need of land and food’; held on 22nd and 23rd November, 2019. Out of 20 participating teams, 10 qualified for the final round. The final round was based on the topic ‘Can India be a secular democracy?’; held on Friday, 29th November, 2019. The students performed well in their 5 minutes speech and 2 minutes rebuttal round. They also asked hard hitting questions to their opponents. This helped them stand as the 2nd Best Debate Team.

Inter School Football Competition
Came second in inter school football competition

Samashti International school boys football team have kept the spirits high by winning the Runners Up position in the 5th Invitational Inter School Basketball Championship held at Hyderabad. Matches were played on a league system where Samashti boys displayed their refined skills by winning against the top schools; Samashti won 4 matches to claim the Runners Up trophy. Harsh Dagar of grade X was announced as best player of the tournament award.

Inter School Drawing Competition
Won third prize in inter school drawing competition

205 students appeared for the Inter School Drawing Competition. 4 students received prizes in group A. 3 students from group B and group C received prizes. All the other students received participation certificate.
